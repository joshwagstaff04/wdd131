Website Planning Document

WDD131 – Personal Budget and Savings Goal Tracker

Site Name

BudgetWise

The name BudgetWise shows that the site helps you manage money wisely. It tracks your income and expenses so you understand where your money goes.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help users track their income, expenses, and savings goals in a simple and organized way. Users will be able to enter income and expense amounts, see totals, and track progress toward a savings goal. All data will be stored locally in the browser so users can return without losing their information.

Scenarios

  • How much money do I have left after paying my monthly expenses?
  • Am I making progress toward my savings goal?
  • Can I come back later and still see my budget without re-entering everything?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2c5aa0 (used for headers and main elements)

Secondary Color: #e8e8e8 (used for the background)

White: #ffffff (used for content sections)

These colors were chosen for their clean, simple appearance and good readability.

Typography

Font: Arial

Arial was selected for its simplicity and readability on various devices.

Reason for Choosing This Project

I chose this project because I want to learn how to use forms and local storage in JavaScript. I also think a budget tracker is something useful that people could actually use, not just a random assignment.

Testing Plan

  • Verify that all pages load and navigation links function correctly
  • Test the process of adding income and expense entries
  • Verify that financial calculations and totals are accurate
  • Make sure the savings goal progress shows correctly
  • Test responsiveness on mobile and desktop devices
  • Verify that data persists after page refresh

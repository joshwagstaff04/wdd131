Summit Strength Fitness – Website Planning Document

This is a living plan for next week’s responsive website build.

Site Name

Summit Strength Fitness — a clear, athletic brand focused on helping people reach their next “summit” through strength training and conditioning.

Optional domain: summitstrengthfit.com (or summitstrength.co)

Site Purpose

The site will help visitors understand programs (strength, conditioning, foundations), view pricing/schedules, learn about the coaches, and contact the gym or book a free intro session.

  • Program overviews with quick benefits and difficulty guidance
  • Schedule & pricing highlights with a clear “Try a Free Session” CTA
  • Coach bios with certifications + short testimonials
  • Contact form and location info

Scenarios (Visitor Questions)

  • “I’m new—what program should I start with?”
  • “What times are classes, and how much does it cost?”
  • “Who are the coaches and what are their credentials?”

Color Schema

Use Summit Navy for headings/nav and Lime Lift for CTAs and highlights. Maintain strong contrast on white for accessibility.

Primary #0E2A47 — headings, nav, buttons
Accent #00C853 — CTAs, highlights
Body Text #1C2430 — paragraphs
Muted #F6F9FC — card/page background

Typography

Bebas Neue for H1–H3 (athletic display), Inter for body text, nav, and buttons (weights 400–700). This combo balances bold headlines with readable copy.

CSS Notes

  • Apply --primary to headings/nav; --accent to buttons/CTAs.
  • Use CSS Grid for program cards; generous spacing and rounded corners.
  • Responsive breakpoints around ~640px and ~1024px.

Testing & Self-Check

  • Validate HTML (no errors)
  • Accessibility: alt text, heading order, keyboard focus, color contrast (AA)
  • Lighthouse: Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, SEO (mobile + desktop)
  • Images optimized and set to lazy-load in the final build
  • Descriptive titles and meta descriptions

This plan may evolve during development and will be updated as needed.
